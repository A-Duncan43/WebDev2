Saber Baseball Code

      Students First – Athletes Second
      Be an example of Integrity and Class:
      You represent yourself, your family, your school, your community, your coaches, and our baseball program – so do it with dignity, loyalty, trust, and pride.
      Always do the right thing . . . especially when nobody is looking.
      Challenge yourself and your teammates to be better each day. Leave each practice knowing that NO ONE out-worked you.
